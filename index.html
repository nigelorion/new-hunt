A HUNT STORY:

Harvey Gorsuch, Owner/Founder of HUNT, began developing his style over sixty-four years ago as a ten year old living in Ferndale, Washington. After spending a summer picking strawberries, he spent his entire wages ($23.00) on three sets of underwear, socks, and an orange and white vertical striped shirt at Kean's Department Store.

Fast forward to the 70's and 80's, where Harvey found himself living in Bellingham but frequently traveling to Seattle to hunt for treasures in menswear thrift shops. His passion for the pursuit of high quality clothing and enthusiasm for menswear resale set him on the path to owning his own shop.

In December of 2016, Harvey opened HUNT Gentlemen's Resale on Edmunds Street in the vibrant neighborhood of Columbia City. HUNT has since become a destination shop in the evolving style scene of Seattle. Located in the Shirley Marvin Hotel Annex, HUNT is a “tiny house” boutique with a cozy arrangement of high end goods.

Hilary Bailey Burnett took her skills to HUNT in August of 2016. As a Personal Wardrobe Stylist, Hilary helps clients find their best selves through the power of good clothes. "Personal style is an accessible luxury. Crafting a wardrobe that elevates daily living is a worthy pursuit".

Harvey and Hilary are purveyors of luxury basics. Their passion for curating inventory will make you a return customer.

ABOUT:

Located on South Edmunds Street in the heart of Columbia City, HUNT is Seattle's premier menswear boutique.

At HUNT, we source gently used designer pieces from the Seattle area to bring you a curated blend of luxury fashion house brands and contemporary designers.

We are deeply committed to the art of resale: reducing waste, elevating investment in quality, and providing access to high end clothing for everyone.

At HUNT, we believe quality #MensWearForAll is a luxury to be enjoyed by people of all gender identities. We aim to dress all sorts of bodies, and provide tailoring recommendations to ensure a custom fit.
